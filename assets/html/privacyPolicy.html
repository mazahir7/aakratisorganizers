Privacy Policy

At Aakrati's Organizers, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website or services.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: When you make a purchase, register on our website, or contact our customer support, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: When you make a purchase, we may collect payment information such as credit card details or other financial information. Please note that we use secure payment gateways to protect your financial data.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our website, including your IP address, browser type, and device characteristics.
  • Usage Information: We collect data about your interactions with our website, including the pages you visit, the products you view, and your browsing activity.

How We Use Your Information:

  • Order Processing: We use your personal information to process and fulfill your orders, including shipping and handling.
  • Contact Support: We may use your information to respond to your inquiries, provide customer support, and address any issues you may encounter.
  • Improving Our Services: We may use data from your interactions with our website to analyze and improve our services, enhance your user experience, and offer personalized recommendations.
  • Marketing: With your consent, we may send you promotional and marketing communications about our products and services. You can opt out of these communications at any time.

Data Security:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. These measures include secure socket layer (SSL) technology for data encryption during transmission and strong access controls to safeguard your data.

Data Retention:

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. When we no longer require your data, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Your Privacy Choices:

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Object to the processing of your personal data.
  • Withdraw your consent for data processing, if applicable.
  • To exercise any of these rights, please contact our customer support.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of these technologies. You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review the privacy policies of these websites for their data collection and usage practices.

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to your personal information or this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: akratisorganizers@gmail.com
