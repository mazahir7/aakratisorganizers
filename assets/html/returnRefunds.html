Return & Refund Policy

Our return policy grants you a 7-day window from the date of receiving your item to request a return. To initiate the return process, please reach out to our customer support team at Aakratisorganizers@gmail.com or call +91 9829169960 They will provide guidance and, if applicable, issue a return authorization.

For a return to be eligible, the item must be in its original, unused condition with tags and in the original packaging. Please ensure you have the receipt or proof of purchase.

Upon receiving the returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of its condition. If approved, we will initiate a refund to your provided account number. Please note, if the wrong product is mistakenly returned, we reserve the right to withhold payment pending further investigation.

To facilitate the refund, kindly provide your account details, including Name, Bank Name, IFSC Code, and Account number, to our customer support team at Aakratisorganizers@gmail.com or +91 9829169960.

Once we confirm the return aligns with our policy, the refund process will commence. You can expect the refund within 10 days from the date we receive the product.

For any concerns or queries about returns, feel free to reach out to our customer support team. We are here to assist you.
